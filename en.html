Never lose a customer service protocol again

Registra is the app that organizes all your service protocols, records calls, and generates reports to help you resolve issues with service providers efficiently.

The Problem We Solve

We've all been through this...

Unresolved Calls

Hours on the phone with customer service without resolving simple problems. With each call, you have to explain everything all over again.

Lost Protocols

Notes on pieces of paper that disappear, forgotten protocol numbers, and no proof of promises made by service agents.

Forgotten Deadlines

Service providers promise callbacks and solutions but never meet deadlines, and you end up forgetting to follow up.

Key Features

Everything you need to document and resolve your customer service issues

Call Recording

Record your conversations to have proof of promises and commitments made.

Protocol Management

Organize all your protocols by company, sector, and type of problem.

PDF Reports

Generate complete documents with service history for formal complaints.

Deadline Reminders

Receive notifications to follow up on resolution promises and callbacks.

Attachments & Documents

Add photos, documents, and important evidence for each case.

Advanced Search

Easily find any protocol by company, number, or subject.

How It Works

Solve your problems in 3 simple steps

1

Log the Service

Create a new record with the company details and the problem to be solved.

2

Document the Calls

Record and note the details of each contact, including protocols and commitments.

3

Track and Resolve

Receive reminders, export reports, and have all the evidence to resolve your case.
