Frequently asked questions

üîí Is my data secure?

Yes. Local files use complete file protection. The Password Vault is secured with AES-256 encryption and supports Face ID/Touch ID. Synchronization is user-based, ensuring complete isolation of your data.

üíé What happens if I upgrade to Registra Pro?

You unlock unlimited case records, the secure Password Vault, professional PDF reporting, full audio recording capabilities, and expand your secure cloud storage to 500MB (with options to expand further via in-app purchases).

üåç Does Registra support my language?

Registra is fully localized and supports 11 languages: English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified), Russian, and Arabic (with full RTL support). Regional formatting for dates and currencies is also included.

üì± Does it work offline?

Yes. Registra uses an offline-first architecture. Your data is saved locally using robust JSON files, and syncs automatically via Firebase Firestore when connectivity returns. You never lose your work.

üìÑ Can I export reports?

Yes. With Registra Pro, you can generate clean, branded PDF summaries of your entire case history‚Äîincluding calls, attachments list, and protocols‚Äîsuitable for legal use or easy sharing with companies and agencies.

üé§ Can I record phone calls?

Yes (Pro feature). You can securely record and link call audio to specific interactions for undeniable proof. Important: Make sure to comply with local laws regarding call recording and obtain necessary consents.

üîÑ How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly in your App Store account settings. You can cancel or modify your subscription at any time, and it will remain active until the end of your billing period.

üéôÔ∏è What are Siri Shortcuts?

Siri Shortcuts let you use hands-free voice commands to interact with Registra. You can say things like "Create New Record," "Open Specific Record," or "Make Quick Call" to speed up your workflow.
