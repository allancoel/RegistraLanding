Frequently asked questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. Everything is encrypted with AES-256 (same as banks use). Your data is yours‚Äîwe can't see it even if we wanted to. Face ID/Touch ID required to open the password vault.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you limited records to try it out. Pro removes all limits: unlimited cases, audio recording, PDF exports, password vault, and 500MB cloud storage. Most people upgrade after their first dispute that needed documentation.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Log a call on a plane, record audio in a basement with no signal‚Äîit all saves locally and syncs when you're back online.

Can I record phone calls?

Yes (Pro feature). Built-in audio recorder for when they make promises over the phone. Just check your local laws about recording‚Äîsome places require you to inform the other person first.

Can I export everything as PDF?

Yep (Pro). Hit export and you get a clean PDF with all your call logs, attachments, and protocols. Perfect for emailing your lawyer or filing with consumer protection agencies.

What languages does it support?

11 languages including English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Russian, and Arabic.

How do I cancel if I change my mind?

Through your iPhone's App Store settings. Cancel anytime, keep using Pro until your current billing period ends. No penalties, no questions asked.
